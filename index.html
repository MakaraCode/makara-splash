How it works

A potential client lands on your website. They're excited and considering a course. Now is the time to book them in.

Schedule, book & pay

  • Your new customer books themselves into an upcoming course listing their pickup location and gear requirements. They make payment effortlessly.

Digital documentation & filing

  • While booking, your customer digitally completes and signs all waivers and documentation.

Notifications & reminders

  • Your customer receives automated reminders via email and SMS. They know exactly where to be and at what time.

Schedule the team

  • With an effortless scheduler, your team know exactly when they're working, where and with whom.

Easy rebooking & spot filling

  • Four days before the planned dive a customer cancels. The first person on the waitlist is automatically contacted. They quickly book themselves into the vacant spot.

POS checkout

  • When arriving at your store for the day of diving, your customer buys a new mask. You process the sale over the Makara POS checkout. With mask stock running low, a draft purchase order is automatically created.

Reporting & integration

  • Explore the profitability of a trip, dive site or even a customer. Integrate effortlessly into your accounting software to save errors and data entry.

Post trip sales and relationship management

  • After retuning home and remicing about a great holiday, your customer decides to purchase the trip photos. They will continue to receive your email campaigns so you never loose the relationship.
